Our Heritage of Excellence

From India's legendary coffee plantations to your cup, we ensure every bean embodies authenticity and premium quality

Crafting Global Connections Since 2025

Pacific International was founded with a singular vision: to bridge the world's finest coffee roasters with India's most prestigious estates. From the verdant hills of Coorg to the aromatic valleys of Wayanad, we've built lasting relationships with plantation owners who share our commitment to excellence.

Today, our beans grace specialty cafes from Tokyo to Toronto, bringing the unique terroir of Southern Indian coffee to discerning consumers worldwide. Our commitment to transparency, fair trade practices, and sustainable sourcing sets us apart in the global marketplace.
